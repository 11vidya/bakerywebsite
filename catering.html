Catering involves providing food and beverage services for events, ranging from small gatherings to large corporate functions. It includes menu planning, food preparation, presentation, and sometimes event management. A successful catering service requires attention to detail, creativity, and the ability to meet diverse client needs. It often includes specialized services such as theme-based menus, dietary accommodations, and personalized service. Catering can be offered in various formats, including buffet, plated meals, or food stations. The quality of service and food presentation plays a crucial role in creating a memorable experience for clients and their guests.
